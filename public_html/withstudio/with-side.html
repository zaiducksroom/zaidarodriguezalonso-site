WITH Side

Explorations of small digital tools designed to support everyday moments.

Concept

WITH Side explores the design of small digital utilities that accompany everyday situations.

The project focuses on subtle tools that support users without demanding attention or disrupting their rhythm.

Experience

The tools are designed to be simple and calm, offering small moments of assistance rather than complex systems.

Each interaction aims to reduce friction and support natural human behavior.

Design approach

WITH Side experiments with minimal interaction patterns and quiet visual language.

The design prioritizes clarity, emotional comfort and lightweight digital presence.

Reflection

The project explores how digital tools can become gentle companions rather than demanding systems.

It reflects on how design can support everyday life without overwhelming the user.
